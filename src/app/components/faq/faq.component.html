What is a Solid Pod?

Solid is a specification that lets people store their data securely in decentralized data stores called Pods. Pods are like secure personal web servers for data. When data is stored in someone's Pod, they control which people and applications can access it.

Why SolidCryptPad?

These days, many documents are written in web based editors such as Google Docs. When using these services, users usually do not have the choice of where their data is stored.

SolidCryptPad offers improvements in terms of security and flexibility given that due to the Solid protocol you can freely choose and switch your storage provider.

On top of that, everything you do is end-to-end encrypted.
